## SECTION 8 – FAQ (INSTITUTIONAL TONE)

### Section Headline
**Frequently Asked Questions.**

### Subheading
Institutional-grade clarity on reserve, redemption, fees, and compliance.

### 6 Institutional FAQs

**1. How is the reserve verified?**
Reserve verification is conducted by independent third parties on a defined schedule. Allocated gold is held in accredited custodial facilities. Reserve coverage metrics are published and on-chain verifiable where applicable.

**2. What are the redemption mechanics?**
Redemption is conducted through authorized partners under defined terms. Minimum thresholds and settlement timelines apply. Contact institutional relations for redemption procedures.

**3. What is the fee structure?**
The smart contract enforces a maximum fee cap of 5%. All fees are disclosed and transparent. No hidden or variable charges beyond the cap.

**4. What governance controls exist?**
Multi-signature controls govern critical operations. A governance plan addresses centralization risk with a path toward progressive decentralization. Upgrade procedures are documented.

**5. Is the smart contract audited?**
Yes. An independent audit was completed July 29, 2025. One Medium, three Low, and two Info findings were identified. All have been remediated. The audit report is publicly available.

**6. Is FDGC available globally?**
FDGC operates in compliance with applicable law. Availability varies by jurisdiction. Institutional and qualified participants should confirm regulatory status in their region before use.
